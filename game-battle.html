F.A.Q

F.A.Q.

If you have any problems contact support, we will answer any questions!

No order in your account?

To receive an order, log out of the account on the store's website, return to the letter with the instructions that came to your mail, click on the "Log in and receive the product" link and go to the "Purchase History" section. There you will find your order.

How to buy?

  • Method 1. Select the product you are interested in, click the “Buy” button, enter the email for the delivery of the order and click “Pay and play”. Then select the desired payment method and pay for the product. The order will come to email immediately after payment.

  • Method 2. Log in to your account or register on the site to replenish the balance for the desired amount, select the product, click "Buy" and confirm the purchase. You receive the order immediately after payment is confirmed. You can view it in your account in the section "Purchase History".

How to activate promo code for the game?

Select the desired product, enter promo code in a special field and click the "Buy" button, enter your email address and pay for the goods.

How to get a promo code?

Enter this promo code to buy any game on the site: SUPERPROMO - the number of uses is limited, hurry up.

What is "Attract luck" and how does it works?

Using this feature, you can increase the chance specific game drop out of case, double drop or top game for an additional fee.

How to replenish the balance?

Register or log in to your account using the "Log in" button in the site menu. Click the "Deposit" button on the left side of your personal account menu, enter the deposit amount, select the desired payment method and confirm the payment. Funds will be credited to your balance immediately after payment is confirmed.

Where to see the purchased product?

After the purchase, you will receive a letter with instructions for receiving the order by mail specified at checkout. You can also view the order in your account in the “Purchase History” section.

How to activate the key?

In the Steam client on the PC, click the “Add game” button in the lower left corner of the screen, select “Activate on Steam ...” and enter the key in the special field in the “Product activation” window.

How to sell the key back to the store?

In the “Purchase History” section, go to the case with the game you want to sell, and click the “Sell Key” button. After the sale funds immediately go to your balance.

I still have questions

With any questions you can contact our technical support. You will be answered within 24 hours.
